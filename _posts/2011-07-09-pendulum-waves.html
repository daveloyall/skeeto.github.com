My grandfather send me this video last week: Harvard Natural Sciences Lecture Demonstrations -- Pendulum Waves. Fifteen pendulums are tuned to oscillate at 51 cycles-per-minute to 65 cycles-per-minutes. It creates interesting aliasing effects for one minute, after which the pendulums come back into sync and the system starts over again.

I thought it was interesting enough to simulate in software, so I made an applet for it,

Click to view the applet
